----------------
Slip Ring Wiring
----------------
red - 5V
black - GND
yellow - CLOCK
orange - DATA
green - MOTOR_1
brown - MOTOR_2


----------
Power Draw
----------
20 LEDs = 0.471 W
20 LEDs + 3.3V motor spinning = 4.2W
